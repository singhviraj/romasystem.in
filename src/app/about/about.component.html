About Us


With the aim of Make in India, we want to deliver our products throughout the world. We, at ROMA SYSTEM, believe in enhancing the quality of our customers as well as our employees through everything that we do. Our aim is to deliver innovative products in a pocket-friendly way. Roma system became the first FMEG(Fast moving electrical goods) company which ensures replacement of electrical products rather than ‘rectifying’ it. Our products are highly appreciated for features like rugged construction, hassle free performance, easy installation, energy efficiency and longer service life. Further, these products are available in various technical specifications in order to meet different requirements of clients.

Contribution towards society as a socially aware corporate citizen


Social and environmental responsibility has been at the forefront of Roma system operating Philosophy and as a result the company consistently contributes to socially responsible activities. For instance, Our company manufactures BLDC(Brushless direct current) fans which uses low energy to run.
